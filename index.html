Our philosophy


Heritage

The first trace of rum distillation dates back to 1651 in Barbados. More than four centuries later, Plantation strives to perfect the long-held traditions of the process.


Tropical Aging

Aged in bourbon casks in a tropical climate, where the Angels’ part is high.


Diversity

Each rum-producing country has its own unique terroir (local habitat) forged with traditions, proven techniques, character, and identity.


Continental Maturing

“One nurtures a rum as one nurtures a child,” explained Alexandre Gabriel on the care devoted to the rums of Plantation.


Double Aging

Two climates, two varieties of oak.


The Raffia Sleeve

A tribute to the history of rum.

Our Rums


Plantation 3 Stars

Freshness and complexity
ABV 41.2%
+ blend of Trinidad rum aged two or three years, young Barbados rum, and old Jamaican rum
Tasting Notes: Nose of baking spices, brown sugar, honey, coffee and cocoa bean. Taste is light-bodied with flavors of dark chocolate, root beer, toasted marshmallow, egg cream, vanilla soda. Refined finish.


Plantation Original Dark

Wealth and authenticity
ABV 40%
+ blend of 3 year old Trinidad rum with Plantation proprietary Bonificateur (15 to 20 year old high esters and petites faux aged rum refined with Ferrand technic
Tasting Notes: Aroma with hints of black peppercorn, clove, cinnamon, baked cherry, molasses, black tea. Taste is fruity with white raisins and dried pineapple, spicy/peppery, and minerally.


Stiggins' Fancy Plantation Pineapple

Character and delight
ABV 40%
+ blend of Trinidad brown rums matured in bourbon casks, infused with Queen Victoria pineapples for three months, and further mixed with a distillate of macerated pineapple rinds and white rum
Tasting Notes: A rich and delicious bouquet, accented by smokey notes and aromas of tropical fruit, pineapple, citrus peel, and a touch of clove. The palate pends with a structured, pleasant sensation of ripe banana, pineapple, and spices layered in a smoky finish.


Plantation XO 20th Anniversary

Quintessential Caribbean
ABV 40%
+ Sophisticated blend of very old rums from Barbados that are finished in many different types of casks ranging from new white oak to carbon cask as well as Cognac casks, each with a different level of toasting
Tasting Notes: Aroma is of intense marshmallow with fragrances of vanilla, cocoa, and milk chocolate. Taste is cinnamon-like and gently sweet, with a profile of holiday spice cake, rum-flavored chocolates, and sugar cookies. Long, delicate, mildly sweet finish.

Signature Cocktails




Mai Tai

1 oz Plantation 3 Stars
1 oz Plantation Original Dark
1/2 oz orange liqueur
1/2 oz orgeat
3/4 oz fresh lime juice
Shake all ingredients in a metal cocktail shaker. Strain, serve over ice in a rocks glass. Garnish with a spritz of vanilla bean infused mezcal and an edible pansie.


Queen Victoria

1 oz Stiggins' Fancy Plantation Pineapple
1 oz rye whiskey
1 oz sweet vermouth
1/2 oz Benedictine
2 dash Angostura bitters
Stir all ingredients in a mixing glass. Strain, serve over ice in a rocks glass. Garnish with an orange twist.


Classic Daquiri

2 oz Plantation 3 Stars
3/4 oz lime juice
3/4 oz simple syrup (equal parts sugar and water)
Shake vigorously in a metal shaker. Strain, serve in a chilled coupe or martini glass. Garnish with a fresh lime wedge.
